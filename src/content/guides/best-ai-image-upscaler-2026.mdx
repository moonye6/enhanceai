If you're reading this, you've already figured out that "best AI image upscaler" is the wrong question. The right question is *best for what subject, at what budget, with what install commitment*.

We tested six tools on the same set of images: an anime portrait, a faded family photo, a game screenshot, a product shot, and a 3D render. Each at 4× upscale. Here's the honest call.

## The contenders

The six tools we tested span the full price and complexity range:

1. **EnhanceAI** (us — $4.90/mo, web)
2. **Topaz Gigapixel AI** ($99 one-time, desktop)
3. **Topaz Photo AI** ($199 one-time, desktop)
4. **upscale.media** (free with limits, web)
5. **Let's Enhance** ($9-50/mo, web)
6. **waifu2x** (free, web — anime-specialized)

We left out Bigjpg (older model, slow), Imglarger (visible artifacts), and Adobe's Super Resolution (locked behind Lightroom — not standalone).

## Quality findings

For modern photos, the gap between the top three (Gigapixel, EnhanceAI, Topaz Photo AI) is smaller than the marketing copy suggests. All three produce results that print well at 13×19 inches.

For anime and illustrated art, waifu2x is still surprisingly competitive on flat cel-shaded work. EnhanceAI's aura-sr model wins on mixed shading and modern digital paint. Gigapixel struggles slightly with line weight preservation on small text bubbles.

For old photo restoration, Topaz Photo AI's "Recover Faces" mode is unmatched. EnhanceAI does well on overall texture recovery but doesn't have dedicated face restoration.

## Price math

If you upscale **once or twice a year**, all the free tools (upscale.media, waifu2x) are fine. Don't pay for software.

If you upscale **5-30 images a month**, EnhanceAI's $4.90/mo is the cheapest paid option. You're spending $0.16-0.98 per image.

If you upscale **50+ images a month consistently**, Gigapixel's $99 one-time wins on amortized cost — about $4/mo over two years.

If you upscale **professionally with face recovery needs**, Topaz Photo AI's $199 is worth it.

## Our honest recommendation

We're biased — but here's the truth as we see it:

- **EnhanceAI** for occasional and prosumer creators (3-30 images/month). Modern model, zero install, transparent pricing.
- **Gigapixel** if you upscale daily and can spend $99 once.
- **Topaz Photo AI** if face recovery and batch are dealbreakers.
- **waifu2x** if you only upscale anime and don't mind the dated UI.
